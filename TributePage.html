About the legend

☛ James Alan Hetfield, was born on August 3, 1963. He is an American musician, he is the lead vocalist, rhythm guitarist, co-founder and a main songwriter of "heavy metal" band Metallica.

☛ He is mainly known for this intricate rhythm playing, but occasionally performs lead guitar duites and solos, both live and in the studio. Hetfield co-foundend Metallica in October 1981 after answering an advertisement by drummer Lars Ulrich in the Los Angeles newspaper.

☛ Mettalica has won nine Grammy Awards and released ten studio albums, three live albums, four extended plays and 24 singles.
